## Bulk Antiferromagnetic NiO

- We start by investigating a simple bulk antiferromagnet - NiO
  - Ni spins up ($Ni_{\uparrow}$, pink) and Ni spins down ($Ni_{\downarrow}$, green) align on alternating [111] planes
  - Magnetic phase contribution is ~500x smaller in magnitude compared to electrostatic contribution
- "Naive" subtraction reconstruction is prone to noise amplification
- By contrast, simultaneous adjoint reconstruction is much more robust, enabling independent regularization
