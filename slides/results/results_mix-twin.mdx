## Mixed Twinned Antiferromagnetic NiO

- Since our technique makes no use of prior-information (notably the use of periodicity)
  - It can reconstruct more complex magnetizations, such as a twinned NiO antiferromagnet
  - And even differentiate between different magnetic orderings for the same twinned structure
